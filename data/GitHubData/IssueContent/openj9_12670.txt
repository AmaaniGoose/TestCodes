We are missing a few Linux on Z feature flags[2], `HWCAP_S390_VXRS_EXT2` and `HWCAP_S390_VXRS_PDE`. These have already been added to the linux kernel [1] but weren't added to the eclipse-openj9/openj9 repo. These flags are needed for `J9PORT_S390_FEATURE_VECTOR_PACKED_DECIMAL_ENHANCEMENT_FACILITY` [3] and `J9PORT_S390_FEATURE_VECTOR_FACILITY_ENHANCEMENT_2` [4] facilities.
